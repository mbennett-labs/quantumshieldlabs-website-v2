"HNDL attacks are likely happening RIGHT NOW ‚Äî attackers storing encrypted data today."

Here's how it works: Nation-state actors and sophisticated attackers are recording encrypted data today ‚Äî financial transactions, healthcare records, corporate secrets ‚Äî and storing it. This is why the a16z report recommends deploying hybrid encryption immediately for any data requiring long-term confidentiality. The timeline debate doesn't matter for HNDL ‚Äî your data is being harvested today.
